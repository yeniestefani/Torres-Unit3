Fairy Tail Characters

Fairy Tail is an anime that follows the story of a young girl, Lucia Heartfillia, on her adventure to becoming a Fairy Tail wizard. She then meets three other wizards, Natsu Dragneel, Gray Fullbuster and Erza Scarlet and an exceed(a flying and talking cat) Happy. Together the four of them create a team and join each other on many adventures and create bonds much too powerful they cant be destroyed.
