Natsu Dragneel is a Fire Dragon Slayer and the main male protagonist of Fairy Tail. He is the adoptive son of a the Fire Dragon Igneel. Natsu is very carefree and reckless causing lots of trouble but when it comes to his friends he is very loyal and protective. He would give his life for his friends. He is also very proud of being the son of Igneel, and everywhere he goes, he has on his scarf that his father gave him. His best friend is the exceed (flying-talking cat) Happy. They are loyal to each other and Natsu loves him. He has many adventures with his friends and is known for destroying many things. he also wants to be an S-Class Mage and many times has challenged the current S-Class Wizards. Though he gets defeated, he never gives up. He is a strong wizard and had defeated many evil wizards. Natsu still searches for his father, who mysteriously dissapeared one day, and gains more stregth everyday.
